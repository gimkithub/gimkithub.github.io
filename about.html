Project-HUB

What is this site?

Project-HUB is a site that acts as a front-end to the carefully curated mass of web-playable games I have collected over the years. From native web games, to unofficial ports of non-web games, we have it all.

It was also designed to have many features that give users freedom of mind when it comes to potential snoopers. For example, the tab can be masked with a custom icon and name.


Why did I create Project-HUB?

Starting back in 2022 during my freshman year of High School, I needed a solution to my boredom, as well as an outlet to develop something. So, I created the very first, very poorly put together, Project-HUB (which was way too popular for what it was).

However, once that the project had exceeded its limit, I decided to finally archive it, and publish the next iteration of Project-HUB. This came with a lot of cool, experimental features, as well as an external host for all of the games and potentially apps added, which is still the backbone of the current iteration.

Unfortunately, that version was left rather unfinished, in an era where my skill had progressed, but hadn't exactly left room for polish. Thus, Project-HUB v2 was put on a pseudo-hiatus, leaving ample time for me to develop the current, and hopefully final version of Project-HUB.
